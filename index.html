About

Hello, thank you for coming to my webpage, here you'll find all (or most) things Josh! From my professional work, to interest subjects, to photography, there is lots to digest. This is a project in my self-taught developer journey, so while it will may feel bare now, come back soon for gradual updates.

I'm from the Pacific Northwest, on the eastern side of Washington. I grew up in a small town with hopes of seeing and understanding the world...I'm steadily on my way, and more and more does this just feel like growing into adulthood.

Part of that is that I don't like being stagnant, continuous learning is a strong passion. Finance, geography, photography, development, are all areas that can yield nuance insights from the diverse types of knowledge that stem from each, the approaches, and in general what shines through the intersectional fields.
